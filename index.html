网站是什么？ What is a website?

网站就像你的 24 小时门店，客户可以通过网站了解您的产品，服务以及业务相关的信息。网站没有时间限制，地点限制，内容限制，通过您的专属网站，您可以无限制的去宣传您的业务，销售您的产品与服务。

Website is your 24 hours 7 days a week sale station. The customers will check your product from the website. With the information on your website, your customer will understand your product, service, and more important your business concept.

There is no physical location limit, time limit, content limit on your dedicated website. You can promote it any time you want anything you want without any limit.
